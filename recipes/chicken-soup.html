Chicken Soup

Above is a bowl of chicken soup

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Carrots
  • Water
  • Celery

Instructions

  1. Brown the chicken in a pot
  2. Remove the chicken, leaving juices in the pot
  3. Add the carrots and celery, cook 2 minutes
  4. Add the chicken back in
  5. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer 20 minutes
